How We Work

From vision to
heirloom

Our process is unhurried, because great woodcraft cannot be rushed. Every piece is a collaboration between artisan and patron.

01
Consultation
We understand your vision, space, and purpose. Every piece begins with a conversation.
02
Design & Sketch
Detailed drawings are prepared, motifs chosen, and dimensions finalized with you.
03
Wood Selection
Premium seasoned teak, sheesham, or mango wood is carefully chosen for each project.
04
Hand Carving
Skilled artisans carve every detail by hand — no shortcuts, no machines for the artistry.
05
Finish & Delivery
Natural oil or polish is applied, and your piece is safely packed and delivered.
